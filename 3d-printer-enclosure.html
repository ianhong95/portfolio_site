Ender 3 Enclosure

March 22, 2026

Using the popular IKEA LACK table enclosure as inspiration, I built my own rendition using fully custom parts.

The enclosure features fully functional doors with integrated magnets, and passthrough cutouts for power and filament. The purpose was to isolate the printer from dust and drafts of cold air that would cause prints to warp.
